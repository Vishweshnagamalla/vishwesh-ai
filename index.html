About Me

I am a passionate Associate Professor and Head of Department for CSE (AI & ML) at Holy Mary Institute of Technology and Science, with over 14 years of diverse experience in both academia and industry. My dedication lies in empowering the next generation of engineers by fostering innovation at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things, preparing them to tackle real-world challenges with cutting-edge solutions. I strongly believe that the destiny of a nation is shaped in the classroom, where teachers serve as leaders and inspirers.
