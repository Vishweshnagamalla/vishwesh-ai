About Me

My career objective is to engage in the noble profession of teaching, where I aim to shape and mold future engineers to build our nation with disciplined technology. I firmly believe that the destiny of a nation can alone be shaped in the classroom, for which the teacher is the leader and inspirer. I have chosen this profession over many industry opportunities, drawing inspiration from Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan.

Currently, I am a passionate Associate Professor and Head of Department for CSE (AI & ML) at Holy Mary Institute of Technology and Science, with over 14 years of diverse experience in both academia and industry. My dedication lies in empowering the next generation of engineers by fostering innovation at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things, preparing them to tackle real-world challenges with cutting-edge solutions. My work focuses on advancing the practical applications of AI in various domains, ensuring that theoretical knowledge translates into tangible impact.
